The Netlify Learning Center & Me

I recently spoke with Daniel Freeman about the opportunity of working for Netlify as the content marketer and developer of the “Netlify Learning Center”. This Learning Center would focus on content that would improve SEO for Netlify in order to gain the attention of new potential users who are searching for topics related to Netlify’s services. I’m a user and fan of Netlify, so I’m excited at idea of creating useful, long-lasting information about it’s services.
